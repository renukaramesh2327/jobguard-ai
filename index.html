🔬 Detection Pipeline

Step 1Text inputPaste job title, company, and description. The more text, the better the analysis.
Step 2Feature extraction5,000 TF-IDF features + 9 meta features (word count, logo, caps, fraud phrases).
Step 3Model verdictRandom Forest outputs fraud probability. Threshold 0.47 → REAL/FAKE/SUSPICIOUS.

🛡️ What We Check

  • PII requests (SSN, bank details, wire transfer)
  • Fees (registration, application, training)
  • Unrealistic pay ($X/day guaranteed)
  • Urgency language (ACT NOW, limited time)
  • Vague requirements (no experience needed)
  • Text length & structure
  • Company logo presence

🚩 Job Scam Red Flags

Signs a job posting may be fraudulent

🔴 High risk

  • Asks for SSN, bank account, or wire transfer before hiring
  • Registration fee, application fee, or training fee
  • "$X/day guaranteed" or "earn $X weekly" with no real job duties
  • Contact only via personal email (Gmail, Yahoo) — no company domain

🟡 Medium risk

  • No company logo or vague company description
  • "No experience needed" for high-paying roles
  • Excessive caps, exclamation marks, urgency ("ACT NOW")
  • Very short or generic job description

🟢 Legitimate signals

  • Apply via careers page, LinkedIn, or known job board
  • Clear requirements (years of experience, skills)
  • Benefits, equity, 401k mentioned
  • Professional tone, company logo, detailed description
